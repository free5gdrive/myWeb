📚 Climate Action Resources

🌱 Practical Tips for Everyday Action

Save Energy

Switch to LED lights, unplug devices when not in use, and use energy-efficient appliances.

Green Transport

Use public transport, carpool, cycle, or walk to cut down on emissions.

Reduce Waste

Follow the 3R’s: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle. Avoid single-use plastics whenever possible.

Eat Sustainably

Eat more plant-based meals, buy local food, and reduce food waste.
