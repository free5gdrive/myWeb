🌍 Educational Section

Causes of Climate Change

🌡️ Greenhouse gases trap heat in the atmosphere.
🌳 Deforestation reduces CO₂ absorption.
🏭 Burning fossil fuels releases harmful gases.
🐄 Agriculture & livestock release methane.

Effects of Climate Change

🔥 Rising global temperatures.
🌊 Melting glaciers & rising seas.
🌪️ Stronger storms & floods.
🌾 Reduced crop yields & biodiversity loss.

Solutions to Climate Change

✅ Switch to renewable energy.
✅ Plant more trees & conserve forests.
✅ Use sustainable farming methods.
✅ Adopt eco-friendly lifestyles.

Quick Explainers

Greenhouse Effect: Natural process, worsened by humans.
1.5°C Warming: Beyond this, irreversible damage.
Carbon Footprint: Total GHGs from your activities.
